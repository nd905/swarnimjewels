Common Questions

How long does approval take?

Our team reviews all submissions within 24–48 hours. You'll be notified via WhatsApp and email.

How do I get paid?

Once a sale is made, your earnings (minus 15% commission) are transferred to your bank account within 7 business days.

Can I submit multiple products?

Yes! You can submit as many products as you want. Each submission is reviewed individually.

What if my product is rejected?

We'll let you know the reason. You can resubmit with improvements or contact us for guidance.

Who handles shipping?

We'll discuss the logistics arrangement after approval — either direct-to-customer or through our warehouse.
