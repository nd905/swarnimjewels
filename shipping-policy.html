Shipping & Delivery Policy

1. Dispatch Time

All orders are processed and dispatched within 24-48 hours of placing the order. Orders placed on weekends or public holidays will be processed on the next business day.

2. Delivery Timelines

We partner with premium couriers (BlueDart, Delhivery, DTDC) to ensure safe delivery.

  • Delhi/NCR: 1-2 Business Days
  • Metro Cities: 3-5 Business Days
  • Rest of India: 5-7 Business Days

3. Shipping Charges

We offer FREE Shipping on all prepaid orders above ₹1,299. A flat rate of ₹80 applies to orders below this amount.

4. Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking link via WhatsApp and Email.
